--Naming Guide--

-Type of Texture-
tile - tile (tiles repeat in all directions, like most brick walls or grass floors)
Trim - trim (trims repeat in one direction only)
Atlas - atlas (basically a collection of textures in one.  Used to save material draw calls. Great for small icons.)
other - f_imp_bed01 is the material name of imp_bed01 in meshes\odem\f\imp_bed01.

Examples:

i_imp_atlas01 would be the atlast for imperial style textures.
w_elv_lsword would be the texture name for a 'weapon' 'elven' 'longsword'.
